Usage: ./player [options]
Options:
--version                Display program version.
--help                   Display the help manual.
--color                  Choose to play rgb-video or not.
--resize                 Resize the video's resolution.
--file                   Choose which video to play.
--fps                    Resize the fps of the video.
-v                       Display program version.
-h                       Display the help manual.
-c                       Choose to play rgb-video or not.
-r                       Resize the video's resolution.
-f                       Choose which video to play.
